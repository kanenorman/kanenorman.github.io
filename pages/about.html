About Me

Welcome to my personal website! My name is Kane and I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Data Science at Harvard University. Before starting my Master's program, I was a football student-athlete at Southern Methodist University where I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics.

After completing my undergraduate studies, I began a career in the tech and finance industries, where I gained valuable practical experience and developed a specialized skill set in math, computer science, and data manipulation. I am passionate about using data to make informed decisions, building data-intensive applications, and using math and technology to transform businesses.

In my spare time, I enjoy staying active, skiing, reading, and watching professional sports. I created this website to share my interests and knowledge in mathematics and data science.
