About Me

Welcome to my personal website! My name is Kane and I am currently pursuing a Master's degree in Data Science at Harvard University. Before starting my Master's program, I was a student athlete at Southern Methodist University where I graduated with a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics. After graduating, I started a professional career in tech and finance where I gained valuable practical experience. Over the course of my career, I have developed a unique and highly specialized skill set with a solid foundation in math, computer science, and data manipulation.

I'm passionate about building data intensive applications, using data to make informed decisions, and leveraging data, math, and technology to transform businesses. In my free time, I enjoy staying active, skiing, reading, and watching professional sports.

I created this website to share my interests in mathematics and computer science. I hope you find the content enjoyable and informative. Feel free to browse and do not hesitate to reach out to me with any questions or comments!
